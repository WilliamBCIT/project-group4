OPERATION COMPOST

FULLSCREEN

Vertical (basic) form

Tower defense game which run on the mobile web browser (also on PC) which raise awareness about food waste reduction. The boy invents towers to prevent foods from going straight into the ‘Waste’ bin. Towers are the real-life examples of food waste reduction and it is to teach the users different ways of reducing food.
